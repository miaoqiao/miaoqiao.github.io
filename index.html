Biography

Miao Qiao joined the School of Computer Science, the University of Auckland, in June 2018. She obtained her Ph.D. degree from the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2013. Her research focuses on the efficiency issue of big data processing, in particular, graph data management, query optimization, social network analysis, stream processing and join algorithms.

Research Interests

  • Graph management and query optimization
  • Social network analysis
  • Join algorithms
